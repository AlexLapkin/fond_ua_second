The Charity Fund "We Are The Future" is collecting donations to create a center to overcome psycho blocks for kids under 7 y.o. in Kyiv. Our team of experienced teachers and psychologists will help children feel better thanks to special classes, art therapy, theater therapy, as well as individual classes with the family.

WHAT WE DO

"We Are The Future" fund was created to help families and Ukrainian children get through the hard time of the war. Kyiv is a large city-shelter where a lot of families live from the occupied territories & destroyed cities from the East and South of Ukraine. Nowadays a large number of children are suffering from war, rocket explosions and the sound of sirens. We decided to help them as soon as possible directly in Kyiv. Currently, we have a project to create a mental and physical care center for children under 7 y.o. from scratch. We want to have a brighter & stable future for our young generation.
